About Atlantia Aquatics

Your trusted partner in the fascinating world of freshwater shrimp

Who We Are

Atlantia Aquatics is a small, passionate business dedicated to providing healthy, vibrant freshwater shrimp for hobbyists across the Philippines. What began as a love for ornamental fish grew into a focus on shrimp, driven by our commitment to quality, care, and sharing the joy of these fascinating creatures with our customers.

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by a passionate aquarium enthusiast, Atlantia Aquatics began as a small hobby with minimal investment and a love for freshwater shrimp.

Starting as a single seller from a modest setup, it has steadily grown into a trusted name among hobbyists in the Philippines. Our journey proves that dedication, passion, and care can turn humble beginnings into something meaningful for the aquarium community.

Our Mission

To provide hobbyists with healthy, high-quality Neocaridina shrimp while offering honest guidance and reliable support, ensuring every aquarium thrives and every customer feels confident in their care.

Our Vision

To inspire a thriving community of aquarium enthusiasts in the Philippines, where sustainable freshwater shrimp keeping is accessible, enjoyable, and trusted by all.
