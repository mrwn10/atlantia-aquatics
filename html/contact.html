Location

We are based in Purok 2, Pansol, Pila, Laguna, Philippines. Atlantia Aquatics is a single-branch setup where every shrimp is raised and cared for with passion.

From a small hobby to a trusted name, our dedication has made us a reliable source for hobbyists who want healthy, vibrant freshwater shrimp.
